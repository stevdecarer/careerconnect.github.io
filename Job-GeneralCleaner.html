Millennial Clean wants to partner with you! Our vision is to serve with the goal of improving lives and businesses. Do you have experience residential, detail or post constructruction cleaning? Here are some of the general duties that would be expected:

Clean and sanitize bathrooms, kitchens, bedrooms, and other living spaces. Vacuum, sweep, and mop floors and carpets. Dust and wipe down surfaces, including furniture and appliances. Change linens, towels, and other household items as needed. Restock household items, such as soap, shampoo, and toilet paper. Report any damages or maintenance issues to the homeowner or supervisor. Follow established cleaning procedures and protocols. Communicate with management, homeowners, property management or host regarding cleaning schedules and special requests. Maintain a professional and friendly demeanor with clients and other team members. ***Tasks may vary depending on the requirements of each client and location.
