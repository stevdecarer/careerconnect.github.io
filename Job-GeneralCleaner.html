Role:

House Cleaner And Pet Caretaker


Full Job Description:

Fulfill Your Passion for Animals and Create a Cozy Home Are you a compassionate and dedicated individual with a genuine love for animals and a knack for maintaining a clean and organized living space? If so, we have an exciting opportunity that combines your passion for pet care and house cleaning.


Company Overview:

We are a household located in Lafayette, GA, just 40 minutes south of Chattanooga, TN, seeking an enthusiastic individual to join our team. We believe that pets deserve the utmost care and comfort, and our home reflects this philosophy. We are looking for someone who shares our commitment to creating a nurturing environment for our beloved animals.


Position Overview:

As our Pet Caretaker and House Cleaner, you will play a vital role in ensuring the well-being of our animals and the cleanliness of our home. Your responsibilities will include, but are not limited to, the following: Cleaning both the interior and exterior of our house, ensuring a tidy and welcoming environment. Letting dogs in and out, providing meals, and maintaining their bedding. Bathing and grooming dogs (prior experience preferred but not required; we will provide training). Assisting with the care of dogs when needed. Utilizing your expertise in grooming, training, or caring for other animals (if applicable).


Position Details:

We understand the importance of work-life balance, which is why we offer a flexible tailored schedule to accommodate various preferences.


Requirements:

To excel in this role, we seek individuals who possess the following qualities: Dedication: A genuine passion for animal care and creating a warm, inviting home.
