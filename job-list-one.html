Role:

The Payroll Manager will manage and mentor our team of 6-10 Payroll Specialists. You will be responsible for training existing employees as well as new hires. You will need to become the SME for our payroll software, PrismHR. You will have your own small book of business so you will always process some payrolls for different clients. You will help to ensure that all payrolls are accurate, timely and compliant with company policies and procedures.


Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Supervision of the payroll team including assisting with the hiring of new Payroll Specialists
  • Training of the payroll team, expanding and cross-training as the company grows
  • Develop and mentor the payroll team, providing guidance and feedback as necessary to improve performance and productivity
  • Assist in all new client implementations ensuring a smooth transition
  • Support Operations, Human Resources and Benefits departments for various payroll related items.
Qualifications

  • PrismHR software experience is preferred
  • At least 5 years of experience in full cycle, multi-state payroll processing
  • Strong knowledge of payroll laws and payroll taxes
  • Solid experience with payroll software systems
  • Desire to take on new responsibilities and work in a challenging environment
  • Customer focused at all times
  • Ability to communicate effectively with all levels of the organization as well as clients
  • Proficient or advanced Excel experience
  • Exceptional attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with a desire to lead and mentor
  • Must be self-motivated, detail-oriented, and able to work effectively under pressure to meet strict deadlines
Benefits

  • Competitive compensation package of $70,000 - $90,000 per year depending on experience.
  • We offer company paid life, dental, and vision insurance, a safe harbor 401k plan and generous paid time off.
If you have what it takes to be a Payroll Manager, we want to hear from you. Apply now and be part of our dynamic team!
